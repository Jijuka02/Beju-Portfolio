About Me

My name is Darcy Mwuhiro known as "Beju". I was born and raised in Burundi. As a passionate documentarian of human stories, my journey as a photographer and illustrator has been deeply rooted in the exploration of life, culture, and the human spirit "Ubuntu". With a background in peace studies and a commitment to amplifying marginalized voices.

My goal is to enable you the freedom to tell your stories, while I create stunning illustrations bringing your work to life.
